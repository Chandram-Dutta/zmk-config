How the mods work

  • Home-row mods: tap for the letter, hold for the modifier. A=Ctrl S=Alt D=Cmd F=Shift — mirrored on J K L '.
  • Cross-hand is instant: ⌘C = hold K + tap C. ⌘S = hold K + tap S. ⌘⇧Z = hold J+K + tap Z.
  • Same-hand needs a beat: for ⌘Z or ⌘V, hold D ~¼s (past the tapping term), then tap the letter.
  • No misfires while typing: mods won't trigger mid-word (150 ms prior-idle guard), and a left mod only activates on right-hand keys.
  • Layers: hold Enter → Nav/Numbers, hold Tab → Symbols, hold both → System (F-keys, Bluetooth, flash).
