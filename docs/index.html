Operating notes

  1. Home-row mods — tap for the letter, hold for the modifier. A ctrl · S alt · D cmd · F shift, mirrored on J K L '.
  2. Cross-hand is instant — ⌘C is hold K + tap C; ⌘⇧Z is hold J+K + tap Z.
  3. Same-hand needs a beat — for ⌘Z / ⌘V, hold D past ~280 ms, then tap the letter.
  4. Misfire guards — mods never fire mid-word (150 ms prior-idle) and only resolve against the opposite hand.
  5. Layer access — hold ENT for nav/numbers, hold TAB for symbols, hold both for system.
